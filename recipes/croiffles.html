TikTok Croiffles

Popularized in South Korea, the croiffle (or croffle) is simply a croissant cooked in a waffle maker. The TikTok-famous treat is the latest addition to the croissant-variant family, next to cruffins, cronuts, and even these stuff-able croissant cones.

TikTok Croiffles Ingredients

  • Puff pastry dough

How to Make TikTok Croiffles

  1. Preheat your waffle iron to medium-low heat, then spritz with cooking spray.
  2. On a lightly-floured surface, roll out the sheet of puff pastry dough with a rolling pin until you get a large rectangle with even thinness. Be careful not to incorporate too much flour. Using a knife or pizza cutter, cut out 8 long and thin triangles.
  3. Place 1-2 tablespoons of your desired filling on the wide end of the triangle and, starting on the wide end, roll the dough into a croissant shape.
  4. Move the rolled dough to the waffle iron, close the lid, and cook for 4-6 minutes, or until golden brown.
